Lodging Information

We hope you can join us for the full weekend at Walker Creek! Ceremonies go by in a blur, and the whole point of this exercise is to spend time with all of you.

There are two options for staying on the Ranch: The bunk houses consist of 4-8 sets of bunk beds per room, but we won't have to pack people tightly into these rooms--there's lots of space to spread out. The semi-private lodging spaces are stand-alone buildings that include a couple beds per room, have bathrooms, and allow for a little more privacy. We'll be establishing quiet zones for families with young children and situating those most likely to keep the after party alive closer to the the event spaces. There may be a small number of fully private rooms available, if you're interested, please write to Daed or Erik to inquire.



Stay tuned for further details about booking your room- for now, please RSVP above!
